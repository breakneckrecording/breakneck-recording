Breakneck Recording Studio

Terms and Conditions

1. No Shoes Inside the Studio

To maintain a clean and comfortable studio, all guests must remove their shoes upon entry. This helps protect the floors, carpets, and acoustic materials from outside dirt and debris. Feel free to bring a clean pair of shoes.

2. No Guns or Weapons

Weapons of any kind are not allowed in the studio—no exceptions. We prioritize the safety of everyone in the space.

3. No Food or Drink Near the Desk

Please keep all food and beverages away from the studio desk and equipment. Accidental spills can cause expensive damage to gear and disrupt sessions. A designated area is available for eating and drinking.

4. Studio Etiquette & Damage Responsibility

Please treat the studio as if it were your own. Keep the space clean and orderly. If you spill something or make a mess, please clean it up. Return things to their place when you’re done. Clients are financially responsible for any damage caused to studio property by themselves or their guests.

5. No Pets Allowed

No animals are permitted in the studio at any time. This is to protect our equipment, maintain cleanliness, and respect those who may have allergies or sensitivities.

6. No Recording of Unreleased Music Without Permission

Recording or playing unreleased tracks you don’t own or have explicit permission to use is not allowed. This protects all parties from potential copyright and legal issues.

7. No Negativity

We strive for a positive, creative atmosphere. Aggressive, toxic, or disrespectful behavior toward anyone in the session or studio staff will not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination of the session.

8. Collection of Session Files

If you need your session files, please collect them within 3 months. Old session files will eventually be deleted to save space.

9. Respect Booking Times

Please arrive on time. Your session begins and ends at the scheduled time, regardless of arrival time.

10. Cancellations

Please provide at least 1 hour notice for cancellations. No-shows or late cancellations will be charged in full.
